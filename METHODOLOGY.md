# KoR Audits — Public Methodology

---

# Methodological Orientation

KoR Audits approaches complex systems as:

```text
constraint-governed adaptive structures
```

rather than isolated optimization engines.

The objective is not to determine:

- truth
- ideology
- morality
- intent
- narrative legitimacy

The objective is to analyze:

- structural coherence
- runtime orchestration
- continuity preservation
- propagation dynamics
- observability surfaces
- stability constraints

under transformation.

---

# Public Methodological Layers

The public audit methodology currently includes:

## 1. Structural Runtime Cartography

Identification of:

- operational layers
- orchestration surfaces
- replay structures
- retrieval systems
- continuity systems
- observability mechanisms

Goal:

```text
understand runtime topology
```

rather than isolated components.

---

## 2. Constraint Surface Mapping

Analysis of interacting constraint families such as:

- latency
- safety
- continuity
- replay stability
- exploration
- infrastructure limits
- economic constraints

Goal:

```text
identify where constraints cooperate,
conflict,
or destabilize one another
```

---

## 3. Runtime Observability Analysis

Study of potentially observable runtime signatures including:

- propagation discontinuities
- replay artifacts
- visibility cliffs
- temporal asymmetries
- exploration bursts
- continuity shifts

Goal:

```text
determine whether orchestration leaves observable structural traces
```

---

## 4. Coherence Surface Analysis

Evaluation of:

- local coherence preservation
- transition stability
- continuity maintenance
- fragmentation risk
- drift accumulation

The framework assumes that:

```text
large-scale systems rarely optimize globally
```

and instead maintain:

```text
locally recoverable operational continuity
```

---

## 5. Continuity & Drift Evaluation

Analysis of:

- symbolic drift
- behavioral drift
- propagation instability
- continuity rupture
- structural fragmentation

Goal:

```text
identify where systems preserve continuity
and where coherence degrades
```

---

# Structural Principles

KoR audits are guided by several principles:

- local coherence
- non-closure
- provenance
- refusal
- runtime arbitration

---

# Epistemic Discipline

KoR Audits separates:

| Category | Meaning |
|---|---|
| Observed | directly visible structures |
| Inferred | plausible architectural interpretation |
| Speculative | higher-order implication |

Maintaining this separation is mandatory.

---

# Public Boundary

This repository intentionally excludes:

- internal tooling
- operational automation pipelines
- proprietary observability systems
- private instrumentation
- executable audit infrastructure

The public repository exposes:

```text
methodological surfaces
```

not the full operational stack.

---

# Final Orientation

KoR Audits should be understood as:

```text
structural systems cartography
```

under constraint.

Not as:

- ideological classification
- certainty generation
- total system explanation

The framework remains intentionally non-totalizing.
